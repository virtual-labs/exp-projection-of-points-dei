PROJECTION OF POINTS

Choose any of the following problems

  1. Draw the orthographic projections of a point A situated 40 mm in front of VP and 20 mm above HP.
  2. Draw the orthographic projections of a point B situated 40 mm behind VP and 25 mm above HP.
  3. Draw the orthographic projections of a point C situated 45 mm behind VP and 30 mm below HP.
  4. Draw the orthographic projections of a point D situated 35 mm in front of VP and 40 mm below HP.
